Have Questions? We've Got Answers

Everything you need to know about our services, purchasing process, and vehicle options

What makes CarHub different from other car dealerships? +
CarHub offers a unique blend of luxury and technology, providing an immersive 3D car viewing experience, personalized service, and a curated selection of premium vehicles. Our platform combines traditional car dealership values with cutting-edge digital innovation, ensuring a seamless car buying experience from start to finish.
How can I schedule a test drive? +
Scheduling a test drive is easy! You can:
  • Book directly through our website by selecting your desired vehicle and clicking the "Schedule Test Drive" button
  • Call our dedicated test drive hotline at +1 (555) 123-CARS ext. 1
  • Visit our showroom during business hours
Our team will contact you within 24 hours to confirm your appointment and provide further details.
What financing and payment options do you offer? +
We offer comprehensive financing solutions including:
  • Traditional auto loans with competitive rates
  • Flexible lease options with customizable terms
  • Special financing for first-time buyers
  • Trade-in options for your current vehicle
  • 0% APR financing on select models
Our financial experts work with multiple lenders to ensure you get the best possible rates and terms tailored to your needs.
What warranty coverage do you provide? +
Our warranty coverage includes:
  • Manufacturer warranty for all new vehicles
  • CarHub Protection Plan for pre-owned vehicles
  • Extended warranty options up to 7 years/100,000 miles
  • 24/7 roadside assistance
  • Complimentary maintenance for the first year
All warranties are transferable and backed by our satisfaction guarantee.
How does your virtual showroom and 3D viewing feature work? +
Our cutting-edge virtual showroom features:
  • 360-degree exterior and interior views
  • Interactive hotspots highlighting key features
  • Real-time color and option customization
  • Detailed specifications and performance data
  • Virtual test drive simulation
The feature works best on modern browsers and devices, providing an immersive experience from the comfort of your home.
What after-sales services do you provide? +
Our comprehensive after-sales services include:
  • Regular maintenance and service checks
  • Genuine parts replacement
  • Custom modifications and upgrades
  • Detailing and ceramic coating
  • Pick-up and drop-off service
  • Loaner vehicles during extended service periods
We're committed to maintaining your vehicle's performance and value long after purchase.
How do I track my vehicle order or service status? +
Tracking your vehicle or service status is simple:
  • Log in to your CarHub account dashboard
  • Use our mobile app for real-time updates
  • Receive automated email and SMS notifications
  • Contact your dedicated service advisor
  • Use our 24/7 customer service hotline
We provide complete transparency throughout the entire process.
What is your return and exchange policy? +
Our customer-friendly policies include:
  • 7-day/500-mile money-back guarantee
  • 30-day exchange policy
  • No questions asked returns for new vehicles
  • Full refund of deposit if financing falls through
  • Transparent documentation process
Terms and conditions apply, but we prioritize customer satisfaction above all.
